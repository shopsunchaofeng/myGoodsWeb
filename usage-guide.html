使用指南

Usage Guide

Hamer 能量糖

Hamer 能量糖为健康食品，采用多种植物来源与营养成分精制而成，适用于成人在特定生活场景下进行营养补充与活力支持。为获得良好的使用体验，请参考以下说明。

食用对象

适合人群：

  • 18 周岁及以上成年人

不适合人群：

  • 未成年人
  • 孕期及哺乳期女性
  • 对产品成分过敏者
  • 有特殊健康状况或正在服用药物者（建议先咨询专业人士）

食用方法

每次用量：

每次 1 粒

食用方式：

将产品置于口中含化，使其自然溶解吸收，请勿直接吞服

食用建议：

建议根据个人作息与实际需求合理安排食用时间，避免一次性过量摄入。

使用时间建议（体验参考）

根据部分使用者的体验反馈：

  • 食用后约 40 分钟左右，身体状态可能逐步进入较为活跃的阶段
  • 如有特定时间安排需求，提前约 4–5 小时食用，体验感受可能更加稳定

上述时间仅作为使用体验参考，不同个体因体质、饮食结构及生活方式不同，感受可能存在差异。

食用频率建议

  • 本产品为浓缩配方，不建议频繁或过量食用
  • 可根据个人情况与生活节奏，合理安排使用频率
  • 建议配合均衡饮食与良好作息习惯使用

储存方式

  • 请存放于阴凉、干燥处
  • 避免阳光直射及高温环境
  • 请置于儿童无法触及的位置
  • 开封后请密封保存，并尽快食用

注意事项

  • 本产品为健康食品，并非药品
  • 不用于诊断、治疗、预防或治愈任何疾病
  • 个体体验可能存在差异
  • 如出现不适反应，请立即停止食用并咨询专业人士

温馨提示

健康的状态来源于长期良好的生活方式，包括合理饮食、规律作息与适度运动。
Hamer 能量糖可作为日常营养补充的一部分，帮助支持积极的生活状态。

重要声明

本页面所述内容仅为一般性使用说明与体验参考，不构成任何效果承诺。
实际使用请以产品包装标示信息为准。

Hamer Energy Candy

Hamer Energy Candy is a health food made from various plant sources and nutritional ingredients. It is suitable for adults to supplement nutrition and support vitality in specific life scenarios. For a good user experience, please refer to the following instructions.

Target Users

Suitable for:

  • Adults aged 18 and above

Not suitable for:

  • Minors
  • Pregnant and lactating women
  • People allergic to product ingredients
  • People with special health conditions or taking medication (please consult professionals first)

Usage Method

Dosage:

1 piece each time

Consumption method:

Place the product in your mouth and let it dissolve naturally. Do not swallow directly.

Consumption suggestions:

It is recommended to arrange the consumption time reasonably according to personal schedule and actual needs, and avoid excessive intake at one time.

Usage Time Suggestions (Experience Reference)

Based on feedback from some users:

  • About 40 minutes after consumption, your body state may gradually enter a more active phase
  • If you have specific time arrangement needs, consuming it about 4-5 hours in advance may result in a more stable experience

The above time is only for reference. Different individuals may have different feelings due to differences in physique, diet structure and lifestyle.

Consumption Frequency Suggestions

  • This product is a concentrated formula, not recommended for frequent or excessive consumption
  • You can arrange the usage frequency reasonably according to your personal situation and life rhythm
  • It is recommended to use it with a balanced diet and good living habits

Storage Method

  • Please store in a cool and dry place
  • Avoid direct sunlight and high temperature environment
  • Please place it out of reach of children
  • Please seal and store after opening, and consume as soon as possible

Notes

  • This product is a health food, not a medicine
  • Not used for diagnosis, treatment, prevention or cure of any disease
  • Individual experiences may vary
  • If you experience any discomfort, please stop consuming immediately and consult professionals

Warm Tips

Healthy state comes from long-term good lifestyle, including reasonable diet, regular work and rest, and moderate exercise.
Hamer Energy Candy can be used as part of daily nutritional supplements to help support an active living state.
