About Hamer Candy

企业介绍｜Company Profile

马来西亚Hamer生物制药有限公司（Hamer Co., Ltd）成立于2007年4月，隶属于马来西亚Hamer集团（Hamer Group Co., Ltd），总部设立于马来西亚，是一家专注于健康食品与生物营养产品研发、生产与推广的企业。

自成立以来，Hamer生物制药始终秉承以科学为基础、以品质为核心、以用户健康为导向的发展理念，持续投入于产品配方研究、原料筛选与生产工艺优化。经过多年的稳健发展，公司凭借稳定的产品质量和专业的服务体系，在全球多个市场建立了良好的品牌信誉。

目前，Hamer生物制药已与多家国际知名的食品企业、健康产品制造商以及批发与零售机构建立了长期、稳定的合作关系，产品远销多个国家和地区，深受合作伙伴与消费者的认可。

产品理念｜Brand Philosophy

Hamer坚信，健康源于细胞层面的平衡与活力。在产品研发过程中，公司专注于研究天然成分与人体细胞营养之间的关系，致力于通过科学配比的营养组合，支持人体日常机能与整体健康状态。

"Hamer汗马糖"正是在这一理念下研发而成，融合多种植物来源成分与营养物质，强调温和、稳定、长期支持的健康管理方式，适用于现代快节奏生活人群的日常营养补充需求。

成分研究与配方优势｜Ingredients & Formulation

Hamer汗马糖选用多种天然来源原料，其中部分核心成分源自传统植物营养研究领域。相关成分中富含多种氨基酸、植物多酚、微量元素及其他天然营养物质，这些成分在人体正常生理活动中扮演着重要角色。

• 氨基酸：是人体蛋白质合成的重要基础，参与能量代谢与多种生理机能的维持。

• 植物多酚及黄酮类物质：具有抗氧化特性，有助于支持细胞健康。

• 三萜皂甙与植物活性成分：在营养学研究中被广泛应用于支持机体免疫与活力状态。

• 人参相关营养成分及水解蛋白：有助于补充日常能量消耗，支持身体耐力与活力表现。

质量承诺｜Quality Commitment

Hamer生物制药始终将产品质量与安全性置于企业发展的核心位置。从原料甄选、生产制造到成品出厂，公司均遵循严格的质量管理流程，确保每一款产品符合相关标准与规范。

公司致力于为消费者提供安全、可靠、值得信赖的健康营养产品，并不断通过技术升级与管理优化，提升产品与服务的整体水平。

展望未来｜Future Vision

面向未来，Hamer生物制药将继续深耕健康营养领域，积极拓展国际市场，持续加强科研合作与产品创新，致力于成为具有国际影响力的健康营养品牌，为更多消费者提供高品质的健康解决方案。

Company Profile

Hamer Biopharmaceutical Co., Ltd (Hamer Co., Ltd) was established in April 2007, affiliated to Hamer Group Co., Ltd, headquartered in Malaysia. It is an enterprise focusing on the research, development, production and promotion of health food and biological nutrition products.

Since its establishment, Hamer Biopharmaceutical has always adhered to the development concept of science-based, quality-centric, and user health-oriented, continuously investing in product formula research, raw material selection and production process optimization. After years of steady development, the company has established a good brand reputation in many global markets with stable product quality and professional service system.

At present, Hamer Biopharmaceutical has established long-term and stable cooperative relationships with many internationally renowned food enterprises, health product manufacturers, as well as wholesale and retail institutions. Its products are exported to many countries and regions, and are well recognized by partners and consumers.

Brand Philosophy

Hamer firmly believes that health comes from cellular balance and vitality. In the product research and development process, the company focuses on studying the relationship between natural ingredients and human cell nutrition, and is committed to supporting human daily functions and overall health through scientifically proportioned nutritional combinations.

"Hamer Candy" was developed under this philosophy, integrating multiple plant-based ingredients and nutrients, emphasizing a gentle, stable, and long-term supportive approach to health management, suitable for the daily nutritional supplement needs of modern fast-paced life groups.

Ingredients & Formulation

Hamer Candy selects a variety of natural raw materials, some of which are derived from traditional plant nutrition research. The related ingredients are rich in various amino acids, plant polyphenols, trace elements and other natural nutrients, which play important roles in normal human physiological activities.

• Amino acids: Important basis for human protein synthesis, participating in energy metabolism and maintenance of various physiological functions.

• Plant polyphenols and flavonoids: With antioxidant properties, helping to support cell health.

• Triterpene saponins and plant active ingredients: Widely used in nutrition research to support immune and vitality status.

• Ginseng-related nutrients and hydrolyzed protein: Helpful for supplementing daily energy consumption, supporting physical endurance and vitality performance.

Quality Commitment

Hamer Biopharmaceutical always puts product quality and safety at the core of enterprise development. From raw material selection, production manufacturing to finished product delivery, the company follows strict quality management processes to ensure that each product meets relevant standards and specifications.

The company is committed to providing consumers with safe, reliable and trustworthy health nutrition products, and continuously improving the overall level of products and services through technological upgrading and management optimization.

Future Vision

Facing the future, Hamer Biopharmaceutical will continue to deepen in the field of health nutrition, actively expand international markets, continuously strengthen scientific research cooperation and product innovation, and strive to become an internationally influential health nutrition brand, providing high-quality health solutions for more consumers.
