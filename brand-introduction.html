产品介绍

Product Introduction

Hamer 能量糖是一款以多种天然植物与营养成分为基础研发的健康食品，甄选多种草本原料，通过科学配比与浓缩工艺制成，旨在为成人日常生活提供温和、稳定的能量与活力支持。

产品配方不含激烈或高刺激性添加成分，强调天然来源与营养平衡，适合现代快节奏生活中对健康与活力有持续需求的人群。

Hamer Energy Candy is a health food developed based on a variety of natural plants and nutritional ingredients. It selects a variety of herbal raw materials and is made through scientific proportioning and concentration processes, aiming to provide gentle and stable energy and vitality support for adults in their daily lives.

The product formula does not contain intense or highly stimulating additives, emphasizing natural sources and nutritional balance, suitable for people with continuous needs for health and vitality in modern fast-paced life.

天然成分 · 科学配方

Natural Ingredients · Scientific Formula

Hamer 能量糖精选多种植物与营养原料，融合传统植物营养理念与现代食品研发技术：

Hamer Energy Candy selects a variety of plant and nutritional raw materials, integrating traditional plant nutrition concepts with modern food research and development technology:

  • 锁阳提取物：传统草本营养成分，常用于活力支持
  • 纯麦芽精华：提供自然碳水来源，支持日常能量需求
  • 咖啡提取物：帮助提升清醒感与专注状态
  • 石榴多酚：富含抗氧化成分，有助于支持细胞健康
  • 人参皂苷：常见于营养补充品中，用于支持体力与活力
  • 水解蛋白：易吸收的蛋白营养来源，补充日常消耗
  • 水果酵素：有助于营养吸收与消化平衡
  • Cynomorium extract: Traditional herbal nutritional ingredient, commonly used for vitality support
  • Pure malt essence: Provides natural carbohydrate source, supports daily energy needs
  • Coffee extract: Helps improve alertness and focus
  • Pomegranate polyphenols: Rich in antioxidants, helps support cell health
  • Ginsenosides: Common in nutritional supplements, used to support physical strength and vitality
  • Hydrolyzed protein: Easily absorbed protein nutrition source, supplements daily consumption
  • Fruit enzymes: Helps with nutrient absorption and digestive balance

多种成分协同作用，形成温和而稳定的营养支持体系。

Multiple ingredients work synergistically to form a gentle and stable nutritional support system.
